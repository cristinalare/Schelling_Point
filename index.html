The Event

We reject the doomerism left to us by the legacy generations & legacy institutions.

We believe that Ethereum is a schelling point for the hopeful & we have come together to build a better world.

Our weapon of choice is novel mechanism design deployed to decentralized blockchain networks.

Celebrate our collective possibility by joining us for this one-day educational event. By co-creating this schelling point, we will reconnect with why we're *actually here* & accelerate these narratives:

Self-sovereignty / Coordination technology / Regenerative economic experiments / Radical markets / Solarpunk lore / & the many fruits of public goods.
